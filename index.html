How it works

  1. 1) Opt‑in
    Choose exactly what to share. No raw audio, no GPS, no email.
  2. 2) Track
    Quick daily check‑ins + HealthKit metrics; optional weekly voice sample (derived features only).
  3. 3) Contribute
    Data is anonymized on export and encrypted in transit to approved research partners.

Privacy by design

  • • No raw audio exported — only derived voice features (e.g., mean F0).
  • • No GPS / IP / email in research exports.
  • • Explicit consent at onboarding; revocable anytime.
  • • GDPR‑aligned anonymization and DUAs with partners.
  • • Local encryption + encrypted transfer.
  • • Transparency — see exactly what leaves your device.
  • • Open docs — public data dictionary and changelog.

FAQ

Is my data anonymous?

Yes. We export only anonymized data: no names, emails, IP, or GPS. Voice exports are derived features only.

Can I opt out later?

Absolutely. You can revoke research sharing at any time from Settings → Privacy.

Who sees the data?

Only vetted research partners under a Data Use Agreement and IRB oversight.

Do you share results back?

We aim for open summaries and community‑readable findings whenever partners can share.
